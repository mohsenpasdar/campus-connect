General Questions

CampusConnect is a dedicated social platform for university students that helps you:

  • Connect with classmates and form study groups
  • Join and manage campus clubs and organizations
  • Stay updated on campus events and activities
  • Share academic resources and collaborate on projects
  • Build your academic and professional network

Unlike general social media platforms, CampusConnect is specifically designed for academic and professional networking within your university community.

CampusConnect is available to:

  • Current university students (undergraduate and graduate)
  • Faculty members and instructors
  • University staff and administrators
  • Alumni (with limited features)

To maintain the platform's academic focus, you'll need a valid university email address (@yorku.ca) to register and verify your account.

Yes! CampusConnect is completely free for all university members. There are no premium features or paid subscriptions. All core functionality is available to everyone with a verified university account.

Account & Profile

Creating a CampusConnect account is easy:

  1. Click the "Login" button in the top right
  2. Select "Sign Up" on the login page
  3. Enter your university email address (@yorku.ca)
  4. Create a secure password
  5. Fill in your basic profile information:
    • Full name
    • Major/Department
    • Year of study
    • Profile picture (optional)
  6. Verify your email address through the link we send you

Once verified, you can start connecting with your university community!

To reset your password:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your university email address
  3. Check your email for the reset link (including spam folder)
  4. Click the link and create a new password

The reset link expires after 24 hours for security. If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, you can request a new one.

You can personalize your profile in several ways:

  1. Click your profile picture in the top right
  2. Select "My Profile"
  3. Click "Edit Profile" to modify:
    • Profile picture and cover photo
    • Bio and interests
    • Academic information
    • Contact details
    • Privacy settings

Remember to keep your academic information up-to-date for better networking opportunities!

Groups & Communities

To join a group:

  1. Go to the Groups page
  2. Browse groups or use the search/filter options
  3. Click on a group to view its details
  4. Click the "Join Group" button

Some groups are open to join immediately, while others may require admin approval. You'll be notified once your request is approved.

You can also discover groups through:

  • Recommendations based on your interests
  • Friends' group memberships
  • Department/faculty affiliated groups

Yes! To create a new group:

  1. Go to the Groups page
  2. Click "Create New Group"
  3. Fill in the group details:
    • Name and description
    • Category (Academic, Social, Club, etc.)
    • Privacy settings (Public, Private, or Invite-only)
    • Group rules and guidelines
    • Group picture and cover photo
  4. Invite initial members (optional)
  5. Click "Create Group" to launch

As the group creator, you'll automatically become the admin with full management capabilities.

As a group admin, you can:

  • Approve or reject join requests
  • Manage member roles and permissions
  • Create and pin announcements
  • Moderate posts and comments
  • Update group settings and information
  • Create events and polls
  • Add or remove additional admins/moderators

Access these features through the "Manage Group" section in your group's dashboard.

Posts & Comments

To create a post:

  1. Find the "Create Post" box on the home page or in a group
  2. Choose your post type:
    • Text update
    • Photo/video
    • Poll
    • Event
    • Document/resource
  3. Write your content
  4. Add any attachments or tags
  5. Select visibility (Public, Friends, Group-only)
  6. Click "Post" to share

Remember to follow community guidelines and group rules when posting.

Technical Support

CampusConnect works best with modern browsers:

  • Google Chrome (recommended) - version 90+
  • Mozilla Firefox - version 88+
  • Microsoft Edge - version 90+
  • Safari - version 14+

For the best experience:

  • Keep your browser updated to the latest version
  • Enable JavaScript and cookies
  • Allow notifications for important updates

Currently, CampusConnect is available as a mobile-responsive website that works great on all devices. Our dedicated mobile apps are under development:

  • iOS app - Coming Summer 2024
  • Android app - Coming Summer 2024

Until then, you can:

  • Add the website to your home screen for quick access
  • Enable notifications in your mobile browser
  • Use all features through the mobile web version

Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when the mobile apps launch!

If you're experiencing technical issues, try these steps:

  1. Clear your browser cache:
    • Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data
    • Firefox: Options > Privacy > Clear Data
    • Edge: Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data
    • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  2. Other troubleshooting steps:
    • Log out and log back in
    • Try a different browser
    • Check your internet connection
    • Disable browser extensions
    • Update your browser

If issues persist, contact our support team with details about the problem and your system configuration.
